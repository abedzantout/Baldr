Cards

Roles and Sizes

Cards are designed to be containers for items that share similar properties but are unique.
There are many different types and sizes of cards and are fully customizable.

Small Primary card
<nga-card> [size]="'small'" [status]="'primary'"> <nga-card-header> <span>Header</span> </nga-card-header> <nga-card-side></nga-card-side> <nga-card-footer> <span>Footer</span> </nga-card-footer> </nga-card>

You can change the role and size of a card by changing the corresponding class name colored in green.
Sizes can be xsmall, small, xmedium, medium, and large.
Roles can be primary, active, disabled, danger, info, success, and warning.
Note that in xsmall cards the footer will always be omitted.

Cards in Layouts

Cards can be used to hold other components such as charts and lists as well.

Header Body Footer
<nga-card> [size]="'small'" [status]="'primary'"> <nga-card-header> <span>Header</span> </nga-card-header> <nga-card-footer> <span>Footer</span> </nga-card-footer> </nga-card>

To use a card as a layout container, you just need to remove the side-section whose tag is <nga-card-side>. To learn more about layouts click here.
